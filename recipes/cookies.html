Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a fun and easy desert they ayone can make. Eat them warm out of oven or pop them in a container and take them on the go. For a gooeyer cook, slightly under bake. Remove from oven when set but before edges brown

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown suger
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1-3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Melt butter in microwave
  3. Mix together butter and suger in a large mixing bowl
  4. Stir in vanilla and egg
  5. Add in all remaining dry ingredients except chocolate chips
    6. , flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Mix into dough
  7. Stir in chocolate chips
  8. Scoop out 1-1/2 tablespoons of dough, medium scoop, about 2 inches apart on large baking sheet
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 7-10 minutes, cookies should be puffy and still slightly under baked in the middle
